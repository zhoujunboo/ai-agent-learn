# Refund Policy

Refunds are issued after the returned item passes warehouse inspection.
Original payment refunds usually complete within 1 to 7 business days depending
on the payment channel. Shipping fees are refunded only when the issue is caused
by the merchant, such as wrong item, quality defect, or logistics damage.

If the order used coupons or points, the final refundable amount should follow
the actual paid amount shown on the order.
